How the simulator works

Monte Carlo analysis, explained plainly.

01
You set your situation
Enter your portfolio, spending, Social Security income, and assumptions about market returns and inflation — all adjustable with sliders.
02
We simulate 10,000 futures
Each run draws random returns and inflation from your assumptions, producing a different outcome. Together they map the full range of plausible futures.
03
You see the honest picture
Not a single projected number, but the full distribution — best case, worst case, and everything in between — so you can plan for reality.
